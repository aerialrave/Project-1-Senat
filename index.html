How to play Senat (modified)

Senat is a two player Game with each player having 5 pieces each, alternating on the top row

Movement and turns are controlled with 4 Paddles that are colored on one side and white on the other.

1 white side up = move 1 spot and gain another throw

2 white sides up = move 2 spot

3 white sides up = move 3 spot

4 white sides up = move 4 spot and gain another throw

4 black sides up = move 6 spot and gain another throw

Game Flow

The game starts with the piece on the top left moving after taking a roll.

Landing on an opponent's piece is an "attack", and you exchange places; you may not land on your own pieces.

you cannot attack a piece if it has an immediate neigbor of the same type, also if 3 enemy pieces are together you cant jump over them unless your jump lands on the next row.

If you land on the part of the map with the jagged lines your piece will be taken up to the ankh or nearest available space.

Land on the 3 dots part of the map and roll 3 white to have a piece removed there.

Land on the part of the map with 2 people and roll a 2 white to have a piece removed there.

land on the wadjet eye and roll a one white to have a piece removed there.

You win by removing all of your pieces.
